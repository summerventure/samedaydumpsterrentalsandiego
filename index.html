Do I need a permit for a dumpster in San Diego?

+

If the dumpster is placed on your private property (driveway, yard), no permit is typically required. However, if the dumpster needs to be placed on a public street or sidewalk, you may need a permit from the City of San Diego. We can help guide you through the permit process and provide all necessary documentation.

What can't I put in the dumpster?

+

Prohibited items include hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, oils), electronics, appliances with freon, tires, batteries, medical waste, and asbestos. We can provide guidance on proper disposal methods for these items. Most construction debris, household junk, furniture, and yard waste are perfectly acceptable.
